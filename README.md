# 🚀 Ghabbour E2E Sales & Inventory Analytics

End-to-end analytics solution built in **Power BI** providing a 360° view of Sales, Brands, Dealers, and Warehouses.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Monitor sales performance, reduce revenue loss, and optimize inventory utilization.

---

## 📊 Dashboards

- **Executive Overview:** Revenue, completion rate, and revenue at risk  
- **Brand Performance:** Avg invoice, brand mix, top models  
- **Sales Team Performance:** Rep performance & execution quality  
- **Dealer Performance:** Top dealers & cancellation risk  
- **Warehouse & Inventory:** Stock levels, utilization, capacity  

---

## 🧠 Data Modeling
- Galaxy Schema (multi-fact model)  
- Used **DAX TREATAS** to create virtual relationships between fact tables  

---

## 🛠 Tools
Power BI | Power Query | DAX

---

## 💡 Impact
- Improved visibility on sales execution  
- Reduced revenue leakage  
- Optimized inventory & warehouse performance  
- Enabled data-driven decision making
